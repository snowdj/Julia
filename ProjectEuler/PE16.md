Project Euler Question 16
===============================
- *2^15 = 32768* and the sum of its digits is 3 + 2 + 7 + 6 + 8 = 26.
- What is the sum of the digits of the number *2^1000*?

<pre><code>
function main(n)
    n = string(BigInt(2)^n)
    println(sum([int(n[i])-48 for i in 1:length(n)]))
end

println(@elapsed main(1000))
